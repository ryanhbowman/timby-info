The slow burn, not the spark

TIMBY is a suite of interconnected digital tools that help teams tackle complex, slow-moving problems with unparalleled speed and security

Problem

Complex problems need long-term solutions

The modern world is rife with gradual plagues – complex, long-term issues like climate change or bureaucratic corruption that periodically flare up into war, disaster, revolution. These big flames and loud booms are easy to see, almost impossible to ignore. But how do we do a better job of understanding the build up to and the aftermath of these moments? How do we get to the root of these chronic problems?

Solution

TIMBY is built to help motivated teams solve big, slow-burn problems over long periods of time

What progress we see in these issues we owe to the most-affected – local communities and organizations that work around the clock to keep difficult problems in their sights. Timby exists to support these teams by offering three distinct digital tools.

Learn more about the tools

Reporting App

Simple, safe and systematic reporting from anywhere in the world

Investigation Dashboard

Help teams to find connections between events that happen across time and space

Storytelling Tool

Share your story with the world in your voice with your reports

In use

TIMBY is being tested by groups across Africa

In Liberia, a renowned activist group uses TIMBY to monitor extractive industries and the spread of Ebola. In Nairobi, a newspaper group has transformed TIMBY into a citizen-reporting tool. In South Africa, groups are building market-based mechanisms to reward users for their contributions in elections reporting. We built TIMBY in close collaboration with its users. Now they are testing and expanding it in so it gets better for everyone.
