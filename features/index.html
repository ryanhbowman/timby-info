Three tools to help teams cope

TIMBY is a suite of interconnected digital tools that help teams tackle complex, slow-moving problems.

Reporting App

An Android app based on Guardian Storymaker made to gather media, text and location data

Investigation Dashboard

A Wordpress and AngularJS dashboard build for moderators to speedily discover links between reports from the field

Storytelling Tool

Cobble reports together into a coherent narrative. Allow anyone to embed and download assets

Reporting App

Android app allows on the ground reporting anywhere in the world

Built-in Tutorials

Video tutorials help even beginners make reports

All Types of Media

Take and send photos, video & audio

Keep Track of Reports

Tag your report by sector, person or company
